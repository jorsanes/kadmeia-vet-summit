# Cookies Policy

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow us to remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

## Types of cookies we use

### Essential cookies
They are necessary for the basic functioning of the website. They cannot be disabled.

**Cookies used:**
- Browsing session
- Language preferences
- Privacy settings

### Analytics cookies
They help us understand how visitors interact with our website, providing information about pages visited, time spent, etc.

**Cookies used:**
- Google Analytics (if configured)
- Performance metrics

### Functionality cookies
They allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language or region) and provide enhanced and more personal features.

## Cookie management

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

### Browser configuration

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Preferences > Privacy and security
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

## Third-party cookies

Our website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website.

## Contact

If you have any questions about our Cookies Policy, you can contact us at info@kadmeia.com.
